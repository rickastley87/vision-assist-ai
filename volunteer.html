Volunteer Opportunities

Support accessible health education and community programming

Why Volunteer with Us?

Volunteering with Light Beyond Sight means helping deliver accessible health education and community programming to blind and visually impaired people nationwide. Our volunteers support production, events, outreach, and more — bringing skills and heart to every episode and gathering.

Whether you can commit a few hours a week or a few days a month, there's a place for you in our community of changemakers.

🎙️ Media

Podcast & video production

2–6 hours per week

Support audio editing, narration prep, show notes, transcripts, and post-production so our health content stays clear and accessible.

  • Audio editing & mastering support
  • Transcripts & accessible descriptions
  • Guest coordination
  • Video narration workflow
📅 Events

Events & live programming

Flexible — spikes around events

Help run accessible health fairs, Q&A panels, workshops, and peer gatherings — in person or virtual.

  • Event setup & registration
  • Live captioning / access coordination
  • Moderator or logistics support
  • Follow-up resources for attendees
📣 Outreach

Community outreach

Flexible schedule

Spread the word in blind and visually impaired networks, partner organizations, and ally communities so people find our content.

  • Newsletter & social support
  • Partner & affiliate outreach
  • Tabling at conferences or calls
  • Listener & viewer surveys
🩺 Clinical

Clinical & subject-matter review

1–3 hours per episode or topic

Licensed clinicians, dietitians, mental health professionals, and advocates can review scripts or episodes for accuracy and practicality.

  • Medical accuracy review
  • Plain-language feedback
  • Resource referrals for show notes
  • Occasional guest expert spots
📋 Admin

Administrative & accessibility

Flexible — remote welcome

Keep operations smooth: data entry, scheduling, email, grant support, and accessibility checks on materials and platforms.

  • CRM & donor communications
  • Grant writing assistance
  • WCAG-oriented content QA
  • Program coordination
🤝 Fundraising

Fundraising & partnerships

2–5 hours per month

Assist with sponsor research, campaign support, and relationship-building so our programs stay free for listeners.

  • Sponsor & donor prospecting
  • Campaign & event fundraising
  • Partnership decks & follow-up
  • Annual giving drives
