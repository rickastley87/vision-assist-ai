Mission & Vision

Health knowledge for the blind and visually impaired community

Our Vision

We envision a future where every blind and visually impaired person has unrestricted, equal access to the health knowledge they need to make confident decisions about their bodies, their care, and their lives — a world where the absence of sight is never the absence of clarity, and where sound, story, and community serve as the bridge to a healthier, more self-determined existence.

We are working toward three horizons:

  • No health knowledge gap. Every blind and visually impaired individual has equal access to health and wellness information — on their terms, in their format, at no cost.
  • A trusted media home. Light Beyond Sight becomes the premier destination for health and lifestyle content made specifically for the visually impaired community.
  • Confident self-advocates. Every listener and viewer walks away equipped to speak confidently with doctors, caregivers, and loved ones about their own health.

Our Mission

Light Beyond Sight exists to bridge the gap between the blind and visually impaired community and the health knowledge they deserve. Through accessible podcasts, videos, live events, and community-driven programming, we deliver trusted health and lifestyle education directly to those who need it most — ensuring that vision loss is never a barrier to living an informed, empowered, and healthy life.
