Health knowledge without barriers

Light Beyond Sight brings trusted health and lifestyle education to the blind and visually impaired community — through podcasts, video, live events, and programming designed so vision loss is never a barrier to an informed, empowered life.

What We Do

🎙️ Accessible media

Accessible health media

Audio-first podcasts, narrated video, and expert-informed content on nutrition, care navigation, mental wellness, and more — built for blind and visually impaired listeners and viewers.

👥 Community

Community & events

Live Q&As, workshops, health fairs, and peer gatherings — in person and online — so education continues beyond the download.

📢 Advocacy

Advocacy & systems change

We work with institutions and policymakers to raise accessibility standards so health information reaches everyone who needs it.
